About

A proud Mancunian Actor-Singer who trained at the Royal Academy of Music in Musical Theatre; graduating in 2019 with a Distinction. She is based in London and Manchester and represented by Lisa Hull Associates. Recent credits include Capucine in Beauty and The Beast at Bedworth Civic Hall directed by Tim Norton, Voices from Across the World at The Cockpit directed by Marcus Fernado and Mae in The Wild Party at RAM directed by Hannah Chissick.

Jas has extensive experience within Immersive and Site Specific Theatre, Childrens Theatre, gigging and touring productions including Macbeth. Prior to this she completed both a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Mathematics with The University of Manchester obtaining a First Class Honours and Distinction and worked as a Software Engineer.

She co-runs Blue Balloon Theatre Company acting as the Associate Director, alongside The Artistic Director Rebecca Pythian. Blue Balloon acts as a platform for actors and others wanting to develop and showcase their own work. Blue Balloon host events such as play developmental workshops, poetry and spoken word open mic' nights, multimedia charity fundraisers and public showcases of talent.
